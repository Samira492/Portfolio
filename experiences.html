Experiences

Civil & Geotechnical Engineer

During my journey as a civil engineer, I have had the privilege of working with renowned companies in the industry, including Betoniran Holding from January 2016 to September 2017 and Behin Tarh Romak (Arman Group) from May 2019 to December 2019. These experiences allowed me to delve into various aspects of civil engineering, honing my skills in areas such as structural analysis , project management, and quality control. I actively contributed to the successful completion of diverse projects, working collaboratively with multidisciplinary teams and stakeholders to ensure the highest standards of construction. The knowledge gained from these positions, combined with my strong analytical abilities and attention to detail, has laid a solid foundation for my current role as a geotechnical engineer in Pardaraz Consulting Engineers since October 2020. In this capacity, I have been involved in geotechnical investigations, site assessments, and the development of comprehensive engineering solutions to address soil-related challenges.
